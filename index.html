Description

Dogde, run, eat, scape, jump and change the gravity so that your microbe survives!

Microbian is an addictive runner, a semi-procedural game in which a microbe flees an organic and hostile world.

By a simple and original control, Microbian keeps the balance between an art game, with an original graphic and sound art, and an addictive platform game developed for iOS.

The arachnid microbe always moves forward. It has the ability to jump and stick to the upper and lower walls of the screen. A tap on the screen makes it jump and a double tap makes it jump and stick to the opposite wall. Sometimes we can get help from friend microbes to jump higher, fly over them or catapult us to avoid dangers.
